About

Andrew Overton is an actor based out of Baltimore Maryland, but due to the nomadic actor life, he hasn't seen his apartment or roommate since June, and wont be seeing either anytime soon. Born and raised in the bustling metropolis of Des Moines, Iowa, which maybe you heard about during last years caucus season, or on the hit TV show, Guy Fieri's "Diners, Drive-Ins, and Dives".

Much like the character Finn from Glee, Andrew spent his formative years pretending he liked sports. It was in middle school that Andrew found a real passion for singing and began taking voice lessons. It was at this point that the Arts grasped ahold of him and refused to let go. His sophomore year of high school, upond seeing a touring production of Wicked (of course it was Wicked) he decided to audition for the school play, and ended up being cast as Damis in an ill-conceived production of Tartuffe.

From then on, this sparked a love for acting and theatre that stayed with him and carried him to Drake University to pursue his BFA in Musical Theatre (after spending one brief semester as a Music Education Major, which according to his mother was "a much more stable profession")

When not acting or writing, Andrew can be found spending too much money on things he doesn't need at your local Nordstrom Rack with a credit card he got "just for emergencies."
